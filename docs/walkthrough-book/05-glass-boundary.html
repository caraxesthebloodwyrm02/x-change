glass-contract-v0

Glass ↔ x-change

Glass sessions name evidence for the EvidenceLedger, but schemas are disjoint. The contract states what Glass supplies — and what x-change will never infer from tool state alone.

In Glass bridge, not present

student_id, reward_id, contract_satisfied, ready_for_payment, student_ack, request_review.

Caller-supplied on ingest

  • session_id from Glass; student_id required from caller.
  • Booleans such as contract_satisfied and ready_for_payment are explicit — no inference from threshold_state.
  • POST /v0/ingest/glass-bridge maps bridge JSON + enrichment to the ingest schema.
Spike status — Typed payload trace, failure paths, and Phase 4 tickets live in INTEGRATION-SPIKE.md. Passive Glass signal updates are documented there.
